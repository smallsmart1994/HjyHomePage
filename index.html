Biography

I am a Lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), and a member of the HFUT-S2AC Group. I received my Ph.D. in the School of Cyber Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Honggang Hu and Assoc.Prof. Bin Liu. Before starting my Ph.D. study, I received my Bachelor's degree from Anhui University (AHU) in 2017. I am broadly interested in Human-computer interaction, Wireless sensing, Wireless communication, Computer Vision, Cyber security, and Internet of Things.
